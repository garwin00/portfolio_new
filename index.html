I'm a Frontend Designer and student from Belfast studying Interaction Design at Belfast School of Art.

I love to design clean, user-focused interfaces with little personal touches that are sure to delight and amuse. My toolbox includes things like gradients (as long as they're purple), grid systems and clean typography with a solid understanding of design thrown on top.

Over the years, I've dipped my toes into pretty much everything - Graphic Design, Photography, Music Production - you name it, I've tried it. No matter the project, I'm able to assist in some way.

Nothing is ever just thrown straight onto the screen without a ton of preparation and planning. Each project starts life as a series of sketches that's continually refined and updated the reflect the constantly changing design process and needs of the individual client.
