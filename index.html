About

I'm a 25 year old Frontend Designer and student from Belfast studying Interaction Design at Belfast School of Art.

I design clean, user-focused interfaces that people seem to love. I like to use clean typography, grid systems and gradients (as long as they're purple) and I'm passionate about all things creative and technical.

When I was a kid I discovered this awesome tool called Notepad and figured out that I could make silly websites for fun and send them to my friends to make them laugh.

After that, I was really into music. Being apparently the only guy in town with access to Photoshop meant local bands soon reached out for me to create gig posters and promotional materials. I decided to merge my love of coding websites with my new-found passion for design and it's all gone up from there.

Over the years, I've dabbled in a ton of stuff like Graphic Design, Photography, Music and beard growth but what's really stuck is my passion for Frontend design. It's just what I want to do. I've never been so sure about something in my life.
