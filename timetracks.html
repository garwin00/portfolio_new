Timetracks

Music through time

About

There's plenty of ways to listen to your favourite music, but a lot less ways to discover new (and old) favourites. My idea with Timetracks (bad name - cool idea) was to make it easier to find music from the past.

Spinning the wheel will take you back exactly a year to see what was released this time last year. All music players are starting to look the same so my idea generation was done on paper first to see if I could do something different. In the end I decided just to keep it simple, leaving the wheel to be the focal point, since it's the base of most interaction. Pressing and holding the wheel will fade the rest of the elements for fine tuning of years. I think it's a pretty cool idea, and I've got some plans for this project.
